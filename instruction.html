Rules And Regulations

1. Click On OPTION TEXT ONLY to Select Your Answer.
2. You can't exit from the Quiz while you're playing.
3. One Point is Granted for Correct Answer.
4. No Negative Marking is Done for Wrong Answer.
5. Once you Select your Answer, it can't be Deselected.
6. You will Have 10 Seconds For each Question.
7. After 10 Seconds New Question Will be Displayed.
8. After Selecting an OPTION wait for the Timer to End for Displaying Next Question.
